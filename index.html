Who We Are

Born in the classroom, rooted in the community.

NutriUIR Club was founded by clinical nutrition students at the International University of Rabat who believe education must extend beyond lecture halls. We connect nutritional science with real-world impact — helping students build healthier lives while raising awareness about food poverty across Morocco and the African continent.

"Access to nutritious food is not a privilege — it is a human right we are committed to defending."
🎓

Student-Led

Founded and run by Master's students in Clinical Nutrition at UIR Rabat.

🥗

Evidence-Based

All advice grounded in clinical nutrition science and peer-reviewed research.
